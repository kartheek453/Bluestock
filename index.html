Frequently Asked Questions?

Find answers to common questions that come in your mind related to IPO.

How to Subscribe to an IPO? -
  • Step 1: Login to your respective service provider.
  • Step 2: Click on the IPO section.
  • Step 3: Select the IPO you want to bid and enter the relevant details.
  • Step 4: Your subscription will be completed once you make the payment or give permission.
Should I buy an IPO first day? +

Buying an IPO on the first day can be risky. It’s better to analyze demand, financials, and company performance before investing.

How do you know if an IPO is good? +

Check the company’s financials, industry potential, demand, and market sentiment before investing in an IPO.

How to check IPO start date? +

You can check the IPO start date on the stock exchange website, company announcements, or your broker’s IPO section.

What is issue size? +

Issue size refers to the total value of shares issued by the company during an IPO.

How many shares in a lot? +

The number of shares in a lot is defined by the company and varies for each IPO.

How is the lot size calculated? +

Lot size is determined based on the issue price and the minimum investment required by the stock exchange.

Who decides the IPO price band? +

Lot size is determined based on the issue price and the minimum investment required by the stock exchange.

What is IPO GMP? +

The IPO price band is decided by the company and its underwriters based on market demand and financials.
